cat

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals that have been companions to humans for thousands of years. Belonging to the species Felis catus, they are known for their agility, independence, and affectionate behavior. Cats were first domesticated in the Near East around 9,000 years ago and held a special place in ancient civilizations, especially in Egypt, where they were worshipped and protected by law.
